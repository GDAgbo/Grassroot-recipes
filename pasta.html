Pasta

Description

This is a renowned recipe known across the globe for its delicious taste and its easy ay of preparing it.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • flavor essence

Steps

  1. Pour a small amount of water into a bowl
  2. Add some flour
  3. Pour some milk into the mixture, and mix well
  4. Add some sugar and flavor essence and mix well for a fine mixture
  5. Pour a little amount of oil into a pan on fire
  6. Fetch some of the mixture into the pan containing the oil
  7. Fry for few minutes, and your delicious pancake will be ready for consumption.
