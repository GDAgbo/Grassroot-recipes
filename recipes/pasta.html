Pasta

Description

This is a renowned recipe known across the globe for its delicious taste and its easy ay of preparing it.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked pasta
  • Cooking pot
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a full, rolling boil
  2. Add an amount of salt to the water, and add the pasta
  3. Stir gently
  4. Cook your pasta at a rapid boil until it is tender but slightly firm to bite
  5. Drain the pasta in a colander
  6. Serve pasta immediately.
